Question Prompt

According to the text, what is the primary physiological benefit of participating in community gardening?

Answer Options

A Improved physical fitness levels
B Reduction in stress hormone (cortisol)
check_circle
C Increased social interaction
D Higher property values in the area

lightbulb Teaching Note

Focus on vocabulary: "cortisol" is a synonym contextually linked to "physiological benefit" and "therapeutic shift". Distractor 'C' is mentioned as a benefit but isn't primary.
